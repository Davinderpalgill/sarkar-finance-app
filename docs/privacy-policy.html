Privacy Policy — Sarkar

Last updated: March 2026

Sarkar is a personal finance tracking app. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Financial transactions — amounts, categories, merchants you enter manually or imported from SMS (Android only).
  • Account information — your name and email used to sign in via Google or email link.
  • Device contacts — accessed only when you tag a person in a transaction or group split. Not uploaded to any server.
  • Voice input — microphone is used only for voice-to-text task entry. Audio is processed on-device and not stored.
  • Photos — camera and photo library are accessed only when you upload tenant documents. Stored in your Firebase account.

How We Use Your Data

  • All financial data is stored in your personal Firebase account and is not shared with any third party.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers.
  • SMS data (Android only) is processed locally on your device to detect bank transactions. SMS content is never uploaded.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device (SQLite) and optionally synced to Firebase (Google Cloud). Firebase is governed by Google's privacy policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Settings screen.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and cloud sync
  • Google Sign-In — optional login method
